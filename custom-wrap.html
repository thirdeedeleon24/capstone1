Custom Wrap

Time for change!

Time for change! From now on your car is no longer bound to keep its one initial color. Thanks to revolutionary wrapping films, you can change the image of your car using whatever graphics you can imagine!

Professional design of the graphic placed on your car will allow you to stand out of the crowd and change the design of your vehicle. Today, carefully selected graphics are considered a kind of art. Such art will make your car unique and one of a kind.

We offer you a professional car styling service

We offer you a professional car styling service with the use of proved and tested solutions. Printing possibilities are amazingly vast – you can adorn your car with characteristic plant ornament, your initials or even your own tattoo. Apart from flat prints we also offer convex printing. After applying the sticker of your choice, we put our special film as a second layer – this gives the impression that the sticker is convex. We get a premium class result – delicate, but individual. Our personalization services give you an opportunity to make your car more attractive by ensuring its unique look.

Graphics can be distributed on the body of the car any way imagined by being adjusted to the individual needs and preferences of our potential clients. You can place them on your hood, doors or any other part of the car – it all depends on your preferences.

Some graphics are more popular among men, others are more frequently chosen by women. One thing is for sure – each is different and capable of giving your car unique and extraordinary look!
