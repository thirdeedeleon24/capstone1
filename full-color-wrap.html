Full Color Wrap

Are you getting bored with your car color?

Are you getting bored with your car color? Or maybe your car has many dents and scratches you wish to get rid of to increase its resale value? You don’t have to think about it anymore. Our innovative service of professional car wrapping will show you how in no time we can change your car beyond recognition! Car wrapping is a service for those who want to change the image of their car effectively and quickly, protecting paint coating at the same time. We use latest generation of special films made with the use of latest technologies and standards.

Car wrapping, apart from color changing…

Car wrapping, apart from color changing, perfectly protects the paint from physical damages, such as abrasions, scratches and rock chips. Films can be placed on any part of the car’s body: bumpers, fenders, side sills or mirrors. The coatings we offer are permanent, solid and resistant to adverse weather conditions. They can be removed or exchanged at any time. Stimulate your imagination and instead of buying new car, change the image of your old one! Professional car wrapping is the simplest way of car transformation and the most effective protection your car can get!
